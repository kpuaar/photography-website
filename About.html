Project 2 - Photography


Description of Project

Project 2 I am creating a Photography website which will allow you to view photos from my most recent trips I have taken either from around the world. On my web page you will be able to view the trips and be allowed to purchase the photos. You can either buy prints of the photos or by downloading them to your computer. Users will be allowed to log into the website and choose what photos they would like to purchase and ones they have already bought.

The languages I will use to create this website will be:

  • HTML5
  • CSS
  • Javascript
  • PHP/Nodejs
