This summer for my 20th birthday we took a trip to Cancun in Mexico it was one of the places on my bucket list to go. It was my first holiday having my own personal camera, so this was a way of me testing out what i was able to do with the Fuji X-T20 and its 16-50mm kit lens.

Airport lounge -This was probluary the first time i ever went to an airport lounge. And i have to say it lives up to the hype. It was just so nice in the busy hustle and bustle of checking in and going through the airport. You get to go to this peaceful calm area of the airport, where you get a chance to sit down and relax before you long haul flight. I think this is going to be a defiant for any up coming flights.

Plane wing -Your typical out of the plane wing photo, i couldn’t tell you where it was taken


hotel club -This was a weird night, we found out on the 3rd night that there was a club in the hotel. We couldn’t believe it how much was in this hotel, like what didn’t they have.
